<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
QuoteMaster Pro, a subscription-based quote management system for SMEs and freelancers. Key features include:
- **Product Catalog:** Manage a library of products with images, units (pcs, m, kg), VAT rates, and prices.
- **Subscription Model:** A Pro membership at 100 TL/month, using Iyzico's Subscription API for payments, with a 7-day trial period.
- **Bank Account Management:** Users can add multiple bank accounts/IBANs and select which ones to display on a quote.
- **Excel Integration:** Bulk import/export functionality for updating product prices via Excel.
- **Quote Generation:** Create professional PDF quotes with accurate calculations (discounts, totals, VAT).
- **Sharing:** Share generated PDFs via WhatsApp and Email.
- **PDF Design:** Minimalist, high-end corporate look with company logo and letterhead design.
- **Customer Management:** A dedicated section to manage clients, who can be searched and selected when creating a quote.
- **Reporting:** A dashboard to track quote status (sent, accepted, pending) and revenue from accepted quotes within a date range.
- **Customization:** Allow users to choose from different PDF templates for their quotes.

**User's preferred language**: Turkish
